About Us

Our mission is to ensure that all of those who walk among us with powers beyond that of a normal human are documented and that those documents are maintained. Here at ASH, we pride ourselves in remaining an unbiased source of free information, accessible to all about superhumans. Our classification as 'hero' is not to be misinterpretted as that of endorsement, persay, our documentation simply echoes public sentiment.
